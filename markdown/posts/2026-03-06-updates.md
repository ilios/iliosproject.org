It's a new year, Spring is springing up, and The Ilios Team is heads down working to execute our plans.

At our inaugural CodeJam for the year in January, we identified our three primary themes of focus for the first half of the year:

- providing robust support for compliance with the new Title II federal guidelines on accessibility which begin on April 26th this year;
- expanding the reporting suite and associated data analytics tools and methods to support on-demand and JIT accreditation needs; and
- aggressively working to improve and refine the overall user experience for all aspects of the platform.

Our goal this year is to take a very good platform, and make it so that it "feels" as good as it is. I don't know if there is a field of study for UX 'vibes', but it is good UX vibes that we are looking to build with this work. We hope you agree and approve of the outcome.

Among the other upcoming targets for improvement: ILM (asynchronous learning activity) session creation and workflow improvements; vocabulary term management improvements; and learning materials upload and management.
